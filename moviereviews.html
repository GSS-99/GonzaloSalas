Best movies of all time

aka: my comfort movies

  1. Grease, by Randal Kleiser

    2. SINOPSIS

    Good girl Sandy Olsson and greaser Danny Zuko fell in love over the summer. When they unexpectedly discover they're now in the same high school, will they be able to rekindle their romance?

    REVIEW

    I've positioned Grease number one for its undeniable impact in pop culture (and for being my ultimate comfort musical movie to watch). From the iconic songs and videoclips, to the cast performance and 50's nostalgia aesthetic. Plus that late 70's film production that makes it so cozy and familiar, the experience of watching Grease is just magical.

    The musical has it all: from funny and energetic songs like Greased Lightning, to intimate ones where we can see some characters showing their most vulnerable side, fx: There Are Worse Things I Could Do. It even has a dangerous car race! And don't get me started with that dreamy surreal ending.. Danny and Sandy saying goodbye in their flying car while We Go Together plays on forever...
